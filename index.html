How to Play

  • Tap the pins that you knocked down on the lane.
  • Click Record Throw to lock them in.
  • Click Gutter if you missed entirely.
  • Your scorecard will automatically calculate strikes (X) and spares (/).
  • Stats are saved automatically when the 10th frame is complete.
